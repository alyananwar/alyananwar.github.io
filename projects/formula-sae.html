Overview

As part of UOP Motorsports from Spring 2024 to Fall 2025, I contributed to the design and fabrication of components for the team's Formula SAE race car. Formula SAE is a student engineering competition where teams design, build, and race open-wheel cars from scratch.

What I Built

  • Designed a dash plate and ECU mount using CAD software
  • Went through multiple design iterations to meet fitment and weight requirements
  • Contributed to the full design-to-fabrication cycle alongside the team

Skills Applied

  • CAD modeling and part design
  • Engineering constraints: weight, fitment, material selection
  • Collaboration on a technical team with real hardware outcomes
