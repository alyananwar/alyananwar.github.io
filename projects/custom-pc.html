Overview

Founded and operate a custom PC building service, working with clients to understand their needs and delivering builds tailored to their budget and use case — gaming, video editing, workstations, and more.

Every build starts with a consultation, followed by component sourcing, assembly, testing, and post-build support. The goal is always a clean, reliable machine the client can actually use.

What I Do

  • Consult with clients on their needs and budget
  • Source and compare components for the best price-to-performance
  • Full assembly, cable management, and BIOS setup
  • OS installation and driver configuration
  • Ongoing support and upgrades after delivery
