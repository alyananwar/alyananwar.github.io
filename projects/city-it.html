Overview

As an IT Support Desk Intern at the City of Stockton (July 2025 – March 2026), I worked across multiple departments supporting the infrastructure that keeps city operations running day to day.

Key Responsibilities

  • Imaged and deployed computers across city departments
  • Installed and configured desktops, laptops, monitors, printers, and phones
  • Coordinated with the network team to clear port security for devices
  • Managed logs and monitored UEM/SIEM systems via Azure and Active Directory
  • Performed secure hard drive wipes for disposal and reuse
  • Resolved tickets and provided remote support via ServiceNow, Bomgar, and KACE
  • Worked with partners including Palo Alto Networks, Telcion, and Quest Software

Tools & Platforms Used

  • Azure Active Directory & Intune (UEM/SIEM monitoring)
  • ServiceNow (ticketing), Bomgar (remote support), KACE (asset management)
  • Cisco Finesse (VoIP), Palo Alto Networks (security)
